Eligibility Criteria

To receive ReframeUrban‚Ñ¢ certification, a project must meet all of the following conditions:

01

Legacy Lot Replacement

The original structure must be at least 50 years old at time of redevelopment.

  • Applies to full teardowns, major gut rehabs, or conversions of obsolete structures
02

Urban Core Location

Property must be located within 3 miles of a major city center or designated transit-rich urban corridor.

  • Designed to promote walkability, reduce sprawl, and reinforce urban density
03

Density Tripling Requirement

The number of dwellings on the lot must increase by at least 3x (e.g., 1 unit to 3+, 2 units to 6+, etc.).

  • Encourages missing-middle typologies (triplexes, ADUs, small multifamily) over megablocks
04

Smart Energy and System Upgrades

The new development must show a minimum 30% reduction in energy use per unit, based on pre-existing conditions or local benchmarks.

  • Smart thermostats
  • Adaptive lighting
  • Water usage tracking
  • Solar integration or EV readiness
05

Environmental Health & Safety Remediation

All legacy material hazards must be fully removed or replaced, including:

  • Lead paint and plumbing
  • Aluminum electrical wiring
  • Asbestos (all forms)
  • Mold, rot, or moisture-related toxins

Documentation of remediation and rebuild must meet HUD, EPA, and local code standards.
